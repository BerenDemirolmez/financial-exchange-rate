About These Charts

These charts show quarterly debt-based financial exchange rate indices for euro-area countries. The metric selector lets you compare asset, liability, and net debt FER.

Aggregate debt FER combines other debt from BIS LBS with portfolio debt assets and liabilities. Sector views cover aggregate, bank, NBFI, NFC, and government.

Method

In the current construction, BIS LBS other debt is treated as the banking-sector component. Portfolio debt assets are based on IMF PIP weights, while portfolio debt liabilities are based on BIS IDS sector and currency information.
